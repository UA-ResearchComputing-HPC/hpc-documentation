# Policies

## Overview

This section outlines the policies, expectations, and administrative procedures governing the use of the High Performance Computing environment and associated services.

These policies are intended to promote fair access, responsible use of shared resources, data security, system reliability, and compliance with university and sponsor requirements. Users are expected to review and follow all applicable policies before accessing HPC systems or services.

Users with questions about these policies or their applicability to specific workflows or projects should contact the HPC support team.
